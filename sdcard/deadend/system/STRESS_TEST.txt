Dead End Inc. stress test pack

This pack pushes the current engine close to its limits.

Expected totals with base content + mods:
- Products: 8
- Districts: 12
- Contacts: 16
- Events: 32

What to test on device:
1. Cycle products in HOME and OPS with PREV/NEXT until all 8 appear.
2. Open MAP and use the page hint at lower right to move across 3 pages of districts.
3. Open CREW and use the page hint at lower right to move across 4 pages of contacts.
4. Play for several in-game days and confirm the event pool still triggers normally.
5. Save, reboot, then verify selected product/district/contact still load correctly.

How to install:
Copy the full /deadend folder from the project SD pack to the card root.
